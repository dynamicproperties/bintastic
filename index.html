How is your service?

We only send kind hearted sweethearts who aim to please.

How big is the bin?

Bins are 40 gallons. They are about 3' long, 2' wide, 18" tall.

Can I use my own bins?

No. We provide free, heavy duty, stackable, standard size bins.

How long is the minimum contract?

One month.

Do I have to sign a contract?

Yes, but the contract is month-to-month. There is no long-term commitment.

Delivery/pickup Trip?

We'll bring you bins, then pick them up. Each round trip (drop off and pick up) is a "trip." This applies to the first time setup and any time you'd like to access your bins. We can haul about 10 bins at a time, so if you have more, it may require multiple "trips."

Can I store anything?

You cannot store combustibles like gasoline, ammunition, etc. You can't store anything illegal.

What happens when I want to access a bin?

Give us a call and tell us the bins you'd like. We'll bring them to you. When you are done, we'll bring them back to storage.

What form of payment do you accept?

Credit card or debit card are our preferred method of payment. We also accept cash, check, or money order.

Is there a security deposit?

No.

Do you have any deals, promotions, coupons, special pricing for veterans, etc.?

No. Our prices are very low, all the time and for everyone.

Can I pay in advance?

Yes.

What areas do you service?

Our standard "Delivery/pickup Trip" fee covers anywhere in the city of Lubbock. If you are outside of Lubbock, give us call to get delivery pricing. Storage pricing will be the same.
